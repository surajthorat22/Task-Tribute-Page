Paul Walker

Paul William Walker IV was an American actor best known for his role as Brian O'Conner in the Fast and Furious franchise. He was born on September 12, 1973 in Glendale, California, and began his acting career in the late 1980s. Walker rose to prominence in the early 2000s, after appearing in the first The Fast and the Furious film in 2001. He went on to star in five more films in the franchise, cementing his place as one of the most beloved actors in the series. In addition to his work in the Fast and Furious franchise, Walker also appeared in a number of other films, including Varsity Blues, Into the Blue, and Takers. Tragically, Walker died on November 30, 2013 at the age of 40, after a car crash in Valencia, California. He is remembered by fans around the world as a talented actor, loving father, and all-around great guy.
